Mission

Committed to promoting quality and equity in higher education that upholds transformative leadership, community-benefited outputs, and research-oriented academic programs imbued with the onward-forward principle of the City of Dasmariñas.

Vision

A transformativeinstituition of higher learning that strategically contributes to the knowledge-based economy of the City of Dasmariñas and proactively responds to the need of the community
